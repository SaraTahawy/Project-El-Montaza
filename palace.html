  1. El Montaza Palace

      2. Features Of El Montaza

    • Soft sandy beaches ideal for sunbathing and swimming.
    • Clear blue waters of the Mediterranean Sea suitable for water sports such as snorkeling and diving.
    • Beachfront cafes and restaurants serving fresh seafood and refreshing beverages.

      Attractions

    • Throne room showcasing royal regalia and artifacts.
    • Reception halls with ornate decorations and furnishings.
    • Presidential suite preserved in its original state.

      Visitors' Experience

    • Guided tours providing insights into the palace's history and architectural significance.
    • Opportunities for photography and exploration of various rooms and gardens.
    • Cultural events and exhibitions occasionally held within the palace grounds
