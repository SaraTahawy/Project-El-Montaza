Table of attractions within El Montaza.

Attractions with Discribtion
Name of Attractions Discribtion
El Montaza Palace Explore the grandeur of El Montaza Palace, also known as Al-Haramlik Palace, featuring opulent halls, royal chambers, and intricate architectural details
Montaza Gardens Take a leisurely stroll through the expansive Montaza Gardens, adorned with lush greenery, colorful flowers, serene ponds, and beautiful sculptures
Beaches Enjoy the sandy beaches along the Mediterranean coastline within El Montaza. Relax under the sun, swim in the clear blue waters, or indulge in water sports activities.
Throne Room Visit the throne room within El Montaza Palace, where royal regalia and historical artifacts are on display, offering insights into Egypt's rich heritage.
Botanical Garden Discover a diverse collection of indigenous and exotic plant species in the botanical garden section of Montaza Gardens, providing a tranquil oasis for nature enthusiasts.
Rose Garden Admire the beauty and fragrance of various rose cultivars in the rose garden, offering a picturesque setting for romantic walks or peaceful contemplation.
Sculpture Park Wander through the sculpture park scattered throughout Montaza Gardens, featuring unique artworks and installations by local and international artists
Historical Exhibits Explore historical exhibits within El Montaza Palace, showcasing artifacts, documents, and memorabilia related to the palace's royal history and significance.
Water Features Marvel at the ornate fountains, cascading waterfalls, and tranquil ponds scattered throughout Montaza Gardens, adding to the charm and serenity of the surroundings.
Outdoor Cafes Relax and enjoy refreshments at outdoor cafes situated within El Montaza complex, offering picturesque views of the gardens, palace, and coastline
