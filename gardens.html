      2. Features

    • Vast expanse of landscaped gardens overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.
    • Diverse flora including exotic trees, shrubs, and flowers.
    • Ponds, fountains, and water features adding to the aesthetic appeal.
    • Walking paths and benches for visitors to enjoy leisurely walks and relaxation.

      Attractions

    • Botanical garden showcasing indigenous and exotic plant species.
    • Rose garden with a variety of rose cultivars.
    • Sculptures and art installations interspersed throughout the gardens.

      Activities

    • Picnicking opportunities in designated areas.
    • Birdwatching for enthusiasts interested in avian species.
    • Outdoor yoga or meditation sessions amidst tranquil surroundings.
