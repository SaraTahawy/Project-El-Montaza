Tips for visitors

  1. How to Get There
    2. By Car:
    Visitors can reach El Montaza by car via the coastal road from downtown Alexandria. There are designated parking areas available within the complex.
    Public Transportation:
    Alternatively, visitors can use public transportation such as buses or taxis to reach El Montaza. There are bus stops nearby, and taxis are readily available in the area
  2. Entry Fees
    3. Palace Tours:
    If visitors wish to explore the interior of El Montaza Palace and join guided tours, there may be an entry fee. This fee typically covers access to the palace's main halls, exhibits, and guided tours by knowledgeable staff members.
    Gardens and Beaches:
    Access to the gardens and beaches of El Montaza may be free of charge for visitors. However, some amenities within the complex, such as beach chairs or water sports rentals, may have separate fees.
  3. Recommended Activities
    • Palace Tour
    • Garden Stroll
    • Beach Relaxation
    • Water Sports
    • Picnicking
